HYDROS

Hyperbaric Underwater Dissolved Oxygen Respirant Station

No tanks. No surface supply. Unlimited bottom time.

NEMO Labs — May 2026

System Overview

HYDROS is a fixed-depth underwater life support station that extracts dissolved oxygen directly from seawater and supplies it continuously to six divers at 20 metres depth. Seawater enters through three axial turbines at 1.5 m/s. A 3.47 m² polysulfone hollow-fiber membrane extracts dissolved oxygen passively using Henry's Law and Fick's Law. Exhaled CO₂ is neutralised by a Sodasorb soda lime scrubber in a closed breathing loop. Clean oxygen is stored in a 228-litre buffer tank and distributed to six divers through a manifold and umbilical hoses.

How It Works

1
Seawater at 20m
Henry's Law
Dissolved O₂ concentration = 7.2 mg/L at operating depth due to increased hydrostatic pressure.
2
Three Turbines
Betz's Law
Axial turbines draw seawater passively at 1.5 m/s, extracting power from natural current flow.
3
Membrane Inlet
Fluid Mechanics
Seawater enters the hollow-fiber membrane vessel through inlet flange at high velocity.
4
Turbulent Flow
Reynolds Number
Flow regime confirmed turbulent with Re = 1,006,500, maximizing O₂ contact with fiber bundle.
5
O₂ Extraction
Fick's First Law
Dissolved O₂ migrates through polysulfone fiber wall via concentration gradient. 20% extracted per pass.
6
Depleted Seawater
Mass Balance
80% of dissolved O₂ remains in seawater as it exits to ocean. No environmental impact.
7
Gas Riser Pipe
Buoyancy
Extracted O₂ rises through vertical pipe to upper chambers via buoyancy and pressure difference.
8
CO₂ Scrubber
Dalton's Law
Ca(OH)₂ + CO₂ → CaCO₃ + H₂O. Neutralises exhaled gas. Lasts 48.5 hours per charge.
9
Buffer Tank
Ideal Gas Law
228-litre tank at 3 atm stores cleaned O₂. Provides steady pressure head to manifold.
10
6-Port Manifold
Fluid Distribution
Gas distributor splits O₂ flow evenly among 6 umbilical hoses. 100mm header, 6×50mm ports.
11
Umbilical Hoses
Fluid Transport
Six 50mm OD hoses deliver O₂ to each diver at 2,400mm length. Each diver breathes continuously.
12
Closed Loop
Conservation
Exhaled gas returns via second channel to CO₂ scrubber. No waste. Zero escape. Perfect cycle.
